## Design Context

### Users
Executive leadership and transformation teams in upstream operations use this dashboard during decision reviews. They need rapid scanability of production health, trends, and risk signals with minimal cognitive load.

### Brand Personality
Decisive, premium, and operationally rigorous. The interface should feel boardroom-ready, data-trustworthy, and calm under pressure.

### Aesthetic Direction
Data-vibrant dark enterprise theme with restrained accents, tight information hierarchy, and intentional spacing. Avoid playful visuals and generic admin-template styling.

### Design Principles
1. Make key metrics instantly scannable with strong hierarchy and contrast.
2. Keep spacing rhythmic and consistent to preserve visual confidence.
3. Use accents sparingly so important values stand out.
4. Prioritize legibility of dense data tables over decorative effects.
5. Keep interactions subtle, fast, and professional.
